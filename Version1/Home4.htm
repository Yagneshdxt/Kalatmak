“Kalatmak” the word itself sounds so creative, so as its meaning! Aesthetics, Artistic & Esthetics.Kalatmak is a very thin line between an Art and the Indian Traditions. And Indian traditions, festivals come with vibrant colours and handicrafts.

At Kalatmak we recognize that urban customers have the power to impact the world simply through their purchasing choices. Our goal is to preserve, revive, and expand the rich tradition of rural handicrafts by working closely with artisans, helping them develop quality products that appeal to contemporary tastes buds.

Our Theme:

Again our name ‘Kalatmak says it all! We just have one theme to follow that is Vibrant colours and creativity of Rural India’.

Yes, our products are made by rural people, however applied the touch of contemporary to get fit in to the urban lives, urban homes.

Our Craftsman’s:

Faction of artistic people from different walks of life; some are house makers with creative instinct & some are qualified interior designers from Sir J. J. School of Arts.

What makes us different?

It is said that wear a black colour bottom, it gels with all kind colours, textures, any type of body structure etc. The same way we, being qualified Interior designers, design product which suit to any kind of décor, paints, textures etc.

We spend hours researching purchasing patterns, design trends and marketing innovations, and use this know-how to help advance the livelihoods of rural craftspeople.
